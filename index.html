What have I done with my spare time

In inverse chronological order.

  • rank-es. Takes links from the front page / pending page from meneame.net (a digg/reddit clone in Spanish) and re-ranks them using the likes and shares scores from Facebook and Twitter. Effectively, it builds a news aggregator site without users. You can see it action at http://rank-es.appspot.com/.
  • kiva-secular. Filters (Kiva)[http://kiva.org] field partners according to their secular and social scores computed by (this team)[http://www.kiva.org/team/atheists]. Results refreshed hourly at http://www.eurielec.etsit.upm.es/~chema/kiva_secular/ when the university servers are up.
